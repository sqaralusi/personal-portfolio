ABOUT

In the flickering light of the theater that is known as my life, I have endured the position of both the protagonist and the playwright, shaping my story through unique experiences.

I’m Shereen Qaralusi, a Collegium V Honors freshman at the University of Texas at Dallas majoring in Healthcare Management. Shadowing medical professionals, I explored the healthcare system, uncovering opportunities for innovation. I’m passionate about family, faith, and the power of healthcare to improve lives. I’m working toward a career where I can lead, innovate, and make a real difference in people’s wellbeing.

This is not the final act, nor the climax of my story. It is only the prologue to something greater. The future will not be defined by those who stand by and watch; it will be shaped by those who dare to lead, challenge, and rebuild. I am here to do just that.
